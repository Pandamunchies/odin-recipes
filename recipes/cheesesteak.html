Cheesesteak

Description

My home town's favorite sandwich is the Philly cheesesteak. a sandwich of beef, choice of cheese, optional onions all on a hoagie roll. My personal order Provolone wit (provolone cheese with onions).

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ribeye steak
  • 1 sweet onion
  • 12 slices of provolone cheese
  • 4 hoagie rolls
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • salt
  • ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Slice rolls, dice onions and chop steak into small thin pieces.
  2. Oil to flat cooktop. Caramelize onions. Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. On separate part of cooktop saute steak and season with salt and pepper.
  4. When both are cooked combine steak and onions on cooktop and separate into four servings.
  5. lay 3 pieces of cheese and a roll on each serving.
  6. When cheese is melted take spatula and flip sandwiches to serve.
