Cacio e Pepe

Description

This is a classic roman pasta dish. The dish is made with simple ingredients of pasta, Pecorino Romano cheese, water and pepper. The finished dish has a taste similar to a carbonara without the eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound bucatini pasta
  • 2 cups grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • fresh ground black pepper(to taste)

Steps

  1. Add salt to water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add bucatini to water and boil about 8 minutes until aldente.
  3. Fine grate cheese into a bowl.
  4. Add pasta water to cheese a little at a time and mix until cheese has a paste consistency.
  5. Add pasta to bowl and mix. Can add more water to make sauce creamy.
  6. Crack fresh pepper onto pasta.
